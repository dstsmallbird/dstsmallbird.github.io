About Me

Greetings!

I’m currently an associate professor at School of Information Science and Engineering, Lanzhou University. With over fifteen years of experience and practice, my research has covered a wide range of areas, including penetration testing, malicious sample generation and detection, and security management. Specifically, my recent work has focused on AI-synthesized speech and open-source security.

I’m from the community and work for the community. Currently, I’m the core organizer of the Lanzhou Google Developer Group (GDG) and a member of the OpenAtom OpenHarmony Security Committee. I also serve on the editorial board or program committee in several journals and conferences. Please feel free to contact me if you have any novel ideas or cooperation plans on exciting security topics.
